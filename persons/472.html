ܐܠܝܐ ܕܨܘܒܐ (Eliya of Nisibis)

975-1046

Metropolitan of Nisibis and author.

Names

  • ܐܠܝܐ ܕܨܘܒܐ
    • Eliya of Nisibis
  • Eliya of Nisibis (Gorgias Encyclopedic Dictionary of the Syriac Heritage, Entry 193)
  • ايليا ابن السني مطران نصيبين (Barsoum (Arabic)/, Page 126)
    • Elijah ibn al-Sani (Barsoum (English), Page 142)

Geography

  • Place of birth: Shenna (North Iraq) (975)
  • Place of study: St. Michael's monastery near Mosul
  • Place of bishopric: Beth Nuhadra 1002-1008
  • Place of metropolitan see: Nisibis ܢܨܝܒܝܢ 1008-1046?

Biography

  • 1002: Eliya was ordained bishop of Beth Nuhadra in 1002.
  • 1008: Eliya was ordained metropolitan of Nisibis in 1008.
