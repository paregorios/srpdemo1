ܓܪܝܓܘܪܝܘܣ ܒܪ ܥܒܪܝܐ (Bar ʿEbroyo, Grigorios)

1226-1286

Maph. (since 1264) and polymath. Foremost representative of the Syriac Renaissance of the 12th–13th cent.

Geography

  • Place of birth: Melitene (1226)
  • Place of death: Maragheh (1286)
  • Place of study: Antiochia/Theoupolis
  • Place of burial: Convent of Mar Matthew, near Mosul

Biography

  • 1246: Bar Hebraeus as bishop of Gubos (consecrated by Patriarch Ignatius II)
  • 1247-1252: Bar Hebraeus as bishop of Laqabin
  • 1252-1264: Bar Hebraeus as bishop over diocese of Aleppo (appointed by Dionysius)
  • 1264: Bar Hebraeus appointed Maphrian (by Ignatius III)
